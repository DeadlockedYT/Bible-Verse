Your Bible Verse


Salvation

If you accept Jesus as your lord and savior, read this prayer:

"Dear God, I admit that I am a sinner, and I need your forgiveness. I believe that Jesus Christ is Your Son and that He died for my sins and rose again. I ask You to forgive me and come into my life as my Lord and Savior. Help me to follow You and live a life that honors You. In Jesus' name, Amen."
